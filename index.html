The Story About OGBC & Worldcoin SEA Launching under Jayden Wei's Support

Our Story

Founded in 2020, OGBC Group has established itself as a premier business consultancy under the leadership of Jayden Wei. With over 12 years of experience in strategic business development and international markets, Jayden brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every client engagement.

This is the story of how OGBC Group, under Jayden Wei's leadership, played a pivotal role in Worldcoin's successful deployment of its first Orb in Singapore — setting a foundation for its broader expansion across Southeast Asia.

Key Achievements

  • ✅ Local Regulatory Navigation :We worked with local legal advisors to ensure the Orb deployment complied with Singapore’s PDPA (Personal Data Protection Act) and MAS (Monetary Authority of Singapore) guidelines. This included preparing Worldcoin’s case for responsible biometric data handling and community engagement.
  • ✅ Operational Setup & Onboarding: OGBC secured prime locations for the first Orb device deployment, trained local operational staff, and introduced key KOLs and early adopters in the crypto community to the product.
  • ✅ Communications & Brand Localization: We coordinated media outreach and adapted Worldcoin’s global messaging to local norms — emphasizing user privacy, financial inclusion, and open-source technology.
